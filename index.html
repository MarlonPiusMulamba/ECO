Mission Statement

To inspire and mobilize communities towards sustainable living, conservation of natural resources, and combating climate change. Through education, advocacy, and direct action, our goal is to protect and restore the environment for future generations, ensuring a thriving and harmonious coexistence between humanity and the planet.

Vision Statement.

A world where nature and humanity coexist in perfect harmony, where clean air, water, and abundant biodiversity are cherished and preserved. We envision sustainable communities thriving in balance with the environment, empowered by renewable resources and mindful consumption. Through collective effort, we strive to leave a legacy of a healthy, vibrant planet for generations to come.

About Us

Welcome to Eco Warriors, a beacon of hope and change founded by three visionary individuals in the dawn of 2024. Our shared dream led us to embark on a journey that intertwines environmental conservation with the noble pursuit of alleviating poverty in local communities. At the heart of our organization lies a commitment to creating a world where the health of our planet and the well-being of its inhabitants coexist harmoniously..

What Sets Us Apart

Community-Centric Programs: Our initiatives are designed with the community at the center. We collaborate with local leaders, listen to the needs of the people, and co-create solutions that are sustainable, culturally sensitive, and tailored to each unique community.

Below are our core objectives;

Reduce Carbon Emissions:

Implement measures to decrease greenhouse gas emissions by a certain percentage within a specified time-frame.

Restore Natural Habitats:

Restore and conserve a specific number of acres or hectares of critical habitats for native wildlife.

More Objectives..

  1. Promote Renewable Energy Adoption: Increase the percentage of renewable energy sources in the local energy grid.

  2. Water Quality Improvement: Achieve specific targets for improving the quality of local water bodies through pollution reduction measures.

  3. Waste Reduction: Decrease the amount of waste generated by a certain percentage through recycling and waste reduction programs.

  4. Tree Planting and Reforestation: Plant a specific number of trees or reforest a designated area to combat deforestation.

  5. Community Workshops and Education: Conduct a series of educational workshops or seminars on topics like sustainable living, wildlife conservation, etc.

  6. Advocate for Policy Change: Successfully lobby for the implementation or modification of specific environmental policies at the local or national level.

  7. Monitor and Report Progress: Regularly assess and report on the progress of conservation efforts, including key metrics and achievements.

  8. Partnership and Collaboration: Establish partnerships with a certain number of organizations, governmental agencies, or community groups to amplify the impact of conservation initiatives.
